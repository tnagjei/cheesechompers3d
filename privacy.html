Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it when you play CheeseChompers3D.

Information We Collect

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data through analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to help us understand player behavior and improve the game experience. This may include session duration, levels played, and general interaction patterns.
  • Cookies: We may use essential cookies for website functionality. Third-party services like Google AdSense may also use cookies for ad personalization and measurement.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email, we will receive your email address and any information you voluntarily provide in your message.

How We Use Information

  • To operate and maintain the game and website.
  • To improve website and game functionality based on anonymous usage trends.

  • To display advertisements via Google AdSense, which helps keep the game free.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services like Google Analytics and Google AdSense. These services have their own privacy policies addressing how they collect and use data. We recommend reviewing their policies for more information.
