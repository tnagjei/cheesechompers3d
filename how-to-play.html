How to Play CheeseChompers3D

Your beginner's guide to controls, objectives, tips, and more!

Game Objective

Your main goal is to guide the mouse through tricky 3D mazes, collecting all the delicious cheese while skillfully avoiding traps and obstacles. Aim for the highest score and unlock new, challenging levels!

Controls

PC / Desktop:

  • Use Arrow Keys (Up, Down, Left, Right)
  • Or use WASD Keys (W-Up, S-Down, A-Left, D-Right)

Mobile / Tablet:

  • Use the On-Screen Virtual Buttons
  • Or Swipe Gestures on the screen

Levels and Difficulty

  • Explore multiple 3D maze levels, each with unique layouts and increasing difficulty.
  • Discover hidden cheese and secret paths for bonus points!
  • Watch out for time limits or special challenges on certain levels.

Power-ups and Obstacles

  • Cheese: The main goal! Collect pieces to earn points.
  • Speed Boost: Grants temporary super-speed.
  • Traps/Obstacles: Avoid these! Contact might cost points or reset your position.
  • Special Items: Look for rare items like invincibility shields or cheese magnets.

Scoring and Rewards

  • Earn points for each piece of cheese collected.
  • Complete levels quickly and find secrets for bonus points.
  • Unlock cosmetic items or titles by achieving high scores (feature coming soon).
  • Climb the leaderboards and compete with friends (feature coming soon).

Beginner Tips

  • Plan your route before rushing in.
  • Learn obstacle patterns to avoid them easily.
  • Don't waste power-ups; save them for when you really need them.
  • Practice makes perfect! Replay levels to improve your skills.
  • Check our blog for more advanced guides.
